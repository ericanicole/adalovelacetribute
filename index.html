Who is Ada Byron, Lady Lovelace?

Ada Byron, born December 10, 1815, is the daughter of poet, Lord Byron. She had many men in her life, but later married the Earl of Lovelace in 1843 and became known as Lady Lovelace. She would go on to become known as the first computer programmer.

A programming language created by the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) was named "Ada" in 1979. "Ada" was created to replace around 450 programming languages in use by the DoD.
